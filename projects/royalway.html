The Royal Way Spiritual Center is a ranch that houses groups interested in meditation. I redesigned the website to reflect the peaceful nature of the organization, focusing on clean and sophisticated layouts to attract younger people.

This project was part of my internship at Willa Creative Agency.

In this project:

Design Process

Deliverables

Design

There were over 10 stages of wireframes. The first few stages were focused on reorganizing the content on the existing website and perfecting the home page layout.

The next set of wireframes included an updated navigation bar and new pages to highlight certain amenities of the spiritual center. I wanted to reduce the amount of text on the home page in order to convey the website as a rental website. I also designed a new "Inquire" page to streamline the process of requesting a visit to the center.

Deliverables

After communicating with the client and team members in the office, the wireframes were sent to the development team to build the website.
