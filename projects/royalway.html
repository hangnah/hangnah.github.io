Spiritual Center

UI Design

Duration

Role

UI Designer

Tools

Sketch

Methods

Information Architecture

Visual Design

The Royal Way Spiritual Center is a ranch that houses groups interested in meditation. I redesigned and shipped the website to reflect the peaceful nature of the organization, focusing on clean and sophisticated layouts to attract younger people.

This project was completed at my fall internship with Willa Creative Agency.

In this project:

Design

Deliverables

Design

I designed over 10 iterations of wireframes.

I was in close communication with the representative of the spiritual center to address their needs for the new website, and frequently received feedback from my manager on my designs. The first set of wireframes addressed the information architecture and reorganization of the content on the existing website. I focused primarily on the home page layout to draw users to the spiritual center.

I updated the next set of wireframes with a refreshed navigation bar and designed new pages to highlight the amenities at the spiritual center. Additionally, I created a new Inquire page to streamline the process of requesting a visit to the center.

Deliverables

After communicating my designs to my manager and the representative of the spiritual center, I shipped the wireframes to the development team to build the website.
