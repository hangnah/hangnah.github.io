Hello, I'm Hannah.

I became interested in webspaces and design in 2007, when I created a WordPress blog to document my love of animals and Club Penguin (which has been set as private to save you from second-hand embarrassment). I’d like to think that my knowledge of writing blog posts and designing graphics has improved since then.

Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, I am studying cognitive science at UCLA, with a specialization in computing and a minor in digital humanities. I have dabbled in graphic design and online marketing at EllieFunDay, organized workshops on design thinking at Design for America (DFA), and assisted in UI/UX with awesome female creatives at Willa Creative Agency. I am currently a UI/UX/Marketing intern at Tutorfly.

I get excited over perfectly curated playlists, seasonal candy cane Hershey’s Kisses, and matcha flavored anything.
