Hi, I'm Hannah –

I became interested in webspaces and design in 2007, when I created a WordPress blog to document my love of animals and Club Penguin (which has been set as private to save you from second-hand embarrassment). I’d like to think that my knowledge of writing blog posts and designing graphics has improved since then.

Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, I am studying cognitive science at UCLA, with a specialization in computing and a minor in digital humanities. I have organized workshops on design thinking at Design for America (DFA), assisted in UI/UX with awesome female creatives at Willa Creative Agency, and served as a UX design intern at two separate online education-related services, Tutorfly and Fulcrum Labs.

I get excited over perfectly curated Spotify playlists, seasonal candy cane Hershey’s Kisses, and matcha flavored anything.

You can contact me here, or find me on LinkedIn, Instagram, or Behance.
